# Jobs to be Done: Visitor

Format: **When** [situation], **I want to** [motivation], **so I can** [expected outcome].

1. **When** I first open the app, **I want to** quickly understand what it does and sign in, **so I can** start applying without confusion or paperwork.

2. **When** I need to visit a government office, **I want to** request a visit from my phone in a few plain steps, **so I can** avoid arranging entry in person or over the phone.

3. **When** I've submitted my request, **I want to** see one clear status at any time, **so I can** know whether I'm approved without calling anyone or guessing.

4. **When** my visit is approved, **I want to** have my entry pass ready and easy to find, **so I can** open it instantly when I reach the gate.

5. **When** I arrive at the gate, **I want to** be verified by scanning my pass, **so I can** get in without explaining myself to a guard.

6. **When** my request is denied or my pass has expired, **I want to** see the reason and what to do next in plain language, **so I can** fix it or re-apply without confusion.
