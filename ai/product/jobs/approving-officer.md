# Jobs to be Done: Approving Officer

Format: **When** [situation], **I want to** [motivation], **so I can** [expected outcome].

1. **When** new visit requests come in for my department, **I want to** see them in one clear queue ordered by urgency, **so I can** work through them without missing a soon-to-arrive visitor.

2. **When** I open a request to review it, **I want to** see the visitor's identity, purpose, host, and requested time in a single view, **so I can** decide without chasing extra details.

3. **When** I've reviewed a request, **I want to** approve or reject it with a plain-language reason, **so I can** give the visitor a clear outcome they'll understand.

4. **When** a question comes up about a past decision, **I want to** look back at what was decided and why, **so I can** stand behind the call without keeping records outside the app.

5. **When** a request was routed to me by mistake, **I want to** recognise it isn't mine to decide, **so I can** avoid approving visits outside my department.
